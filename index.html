About Me

Here's what I'm about.

My name is Alfie Santos and I’m a web developer. Although I started out in the humanities, majoring in Spanish and Russian before earning an MA in philosophy, I felt the need to descend from the abstract, theoretical clouds and start digging into the concrete, technical weeds. I turned down scholarships to law school because I believe that neither law nor politics has the power to shape the world to the degree that tech has. This field will improve the lives of individuals and transform entire societies in new and exciting ways, and I want to be a part of it. In short, I’m taking to heart the following exhortation of a well-known German thinker:


"Philosophers have hitherto only interpreted the world in various ways; the point is to change it."


Although I did not originally start out on this path, I believe my background makes me well suited to it. After all, a programming language is essentially a language of logic. I have spent many years learning natural languages, and philosophy is fundamentally based on logic. I enjoy solving problems, overcoming challenges, and building things that people will actually use, and I look forward to continuing to develop my programming skills and immersing myself in this complex, fascinating field.
